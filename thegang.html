The Gang

Arthur Morgan

You Don't Get To Live A Bad Life And Have Good Things Happen To You.

The protagonist of the game. A rugged outlaw and a member of the Van der Linde gang, known for his loyalty and sense of honor.

Dutch Van Der Linde

Who Amongst You Is With Me, And Who Is Betraying Me?

The charismatic but morally ambiguous leader of the Van der Linde gang, known for his eloquent speeches and persuasive charm.

Hosea Matthews

My Pa used to say if you stare into the fire long enough, you can see the whole world pass by.

Known for his intelligence, wisdom, and his role as the gang's con artist and strategist, offering guidance to the protagonist, Arthur Morgan, throughout the game.

John Marston

I am always honest, maybe not always good, but I'm always honest.

He is a loyal and pragmatic member of the Van der Linde gang, known for his proficiency in combat and his unwavering commitment to the gang's leader, Dutch van der Linde.

Sadie Adler

You're About To Have A Problem With Getting Punched In The Face!

A fierce and independent woman who becomes a member of the Van der Linde gang after a tragic event leaves her widowed and seeking revenge.

Javier Escuella

For this, this is my home now. You...you are all my home now.

A loyal and skilled member of the Van der Linde gang, specializing in handling horses and contributing to the gang's various criminal activities. He is a Mexican outlaw who is deeply connected to Dutch van der Linde and the gang's history.

Charles Smith

I dont kill for fun I kill when I need too

Known for his calm and level-headed nature, as well as his exceptional hunting and tracking skills. Throughout the game, he forms a close bond with the protagonist, Arthur Morgan.

Bill Williamson

Do I look like I need saving?

A loyal member of Dutch's gang that serves as an enforcer and muscle for the group. He is often involved in the gang's various criminal activities, including robberies, heists, and shootouts.

Uncle

Oh, you don't build a barn, dumbass. What do you think this is, 1785?

Uncle is often seen lounging around the camp, avoiding work and generally being a nuisance to the gang. Despite his lazy demeanor, he occasionally provides comic relief and serves as a reminder of the gang's dysfunctional dynamics.

Lenny Summers

I've been runnin from every lawman and bounty hunter west of the Lannahecee River.

Lenny is one of the newer recruits to the gang and is known for his optimism, youthful energy, and desire to prove himself as a capable outlaw.

Micha Bell

Dutch Always Said You Were A Big Shadow Cast By A Tiny Tree.

A significant antagonist who disrupts the gang's unity and often betrays his comrades for his own interests. Unlike many other members of the gang, Micah is primarily loyal to himself and his own interests.

Sean Macquire

I see you fondling that new scar of yours like you're gonna buy it breakfast in the mornin.

An Irishman who is known for his humorous and jovial personality. He's often the source of comic relief within the group, and his Irish accent and quick wit make him a memorable character in the game.

Abigail Roberts

We all dreamt a lot. Then we grew into women. Saw what the world was for us. How little it values us.

A loyal and resilient member of the gang. She's fiercely devoted to her partner, John Marston, and their son, Jack. Abigail is a motherly figure to the younger members of the gang, and she often provides guidance and care, particularly to Jack and other vulnerable individuals in the group.

Jack Marston

Did you catch the bad guys?

He is the son of John Marston and Abigail Roberts. He was born into a life of outlaws and is often sheltered from the more violent aspects of the gang's activities.

Pearson

Pearson is responsible for the gang's food preparation and hunting. He ensures that the gang members are well-fed by sourcing and cooking food. He is known for his work ethic and his no-nonsense approach to his responsibilities.

Susan Grimshaw

If they've touched a single hair on that girl's head, I will eviscerate the sons of bitches.

The camp's enforcer and caretaker. She ensures that the gang's members follow Dutch's orders and maintain order within the camp. She has a reputation for being stern and keeping the gang members in line, often serving as a maternal figure within the group.

Reverend Swanson

You have to love yourself a fire.

A complex character in the game that is responsible for providing spiritual guidance to the gang members. Swanson is offering a unique perspective on spirituality and morality in the harsh, morally ambiguous world of the Old West.
